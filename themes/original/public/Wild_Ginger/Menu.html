Style of Food


Wild Ginger serves traditional Balinese food and also some of Putu’s original specials! You can enjoy a wonderful feast, or something more simple. She also has a secret menu for regulars, so please come often! You may be lucky enough to get a taste of it if you ask nicely.

Atmosphere


Prepare to smile and enjoy the wonderfully calm ambiance of Wild Ginger. You are guaranteed to enjoy Balinese hospitality and take pleasure in some great conversations.
