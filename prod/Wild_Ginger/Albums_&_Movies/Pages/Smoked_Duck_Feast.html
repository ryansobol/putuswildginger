This is one of the items on Putu’s secret Menu! This item must be ordered at least one day in advance. When you order the Smoked Duck Putu will also make you the special dishes that she only makes with that dish. The Duck is wrapped in a banana leaf and left to smoke in its marinades and juices, underground, for 24 hours. Also not on the Menu which is included in the meal is the Chicken Satay, and her special Balinese Green Beans and Soy beans. What is on the Menu is the Mixed Salad, Tofu Curry, and White Rice. All in all, this meal, becomes a Feast (for the eyes and the stomach.) So save room, and as Putu says, “Eat slowly!”
